NATIONAL GEOGRAPHIC WILD
This project focused on designing a visual package for a television channel of choice, combining creativity with technical execution. The aim was to refresh the channel’s image using video, photography, sound, typography, 2D and 3D graphics, while staying aligned with its audience and market. I chose “National Geographic Wild” due to my interest in documentaries, wildlife, and nature, which offered a visually rich and inspiring context to explore.
