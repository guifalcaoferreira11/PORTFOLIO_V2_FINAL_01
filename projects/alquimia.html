ALQUIMIA
Rebranding concept for Alquimia, a wellness and spa brand rooted in nature and ritual. Inspired by alchemy and the elements of the earth, the rebrand positions the brand as an integrated experience that goes beyond appearance, focusing on transformation, tranquility, and inner harmony. The identity reframes each visit as a moment of pause and renewal, connecting body, nature, and balance through a cohesive and intentional approach.
