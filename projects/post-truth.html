POST TRUTH
The project connects closely with the evolving role of artificial intelligence in creative fields. Just as AI has revolutionized artistic expression and visual creation, it also plays a crucial role in the production and dissemination of information, including misinformation. By examining the rise of “fake news” we recognized how technological tools, including AI-driven algorithms, contribute to the spread of false narratives. The goal is to shift from a passive to an active stance, helping ensure that truth prevails in a digital world full of manipulation.
