BEASTYBITES
BeastyBites is a playful, sustainable packaging concept for dehydrated fruit snacks made from 100% natural ingredients. Designed for children, it combines nutrition with fun by transforming into a customizable, interactive toy that kids can build and collect. Bold colors, hand-drawn characters, and a modular structure encourage creativity beyond consumption. Made from recyclable paperboard with plant-based coatings and eco-friendly inks, it promotes environmental responsibility while delivering a healthy and engaging snack experience.
